    Our Story

    Our Journey

    Every family has a unique journey, and ours is filled with love, laughter, and countless precious moments. From our roots in China to building our lives in the Greater Seattle Area, we've created a home filled with technology, innovation, and family values. Each chapter has shaped who we are as a family, and we're excited for all the adventures ahead.

    Family Values

    What We Believe In

    We treat each other with kindness, understanding, and unconditional love. Our family is built on mutual respect and support for one another.

    We stand together through thick and thin. Whether celebrating successes or facing challenges, we face life as a team.

    We keep open and honest dialogue, sharing our thoughts, dreams, and concerns with each other.

    We celebrate our Chinese roots and pass down our traditions while embracing our American experiences.

    We invest in education, personal growth, and building a better tomorrow for the next generation.
