    Empowering communities through education, innovation, and compassionate giving.

## About the Foundation

The Li Family Foundation is dedicated to making a positive impact through strategic philanthropy, community engagement, and support for causes that align with our values. We believe in giving back and creating opportunities for others to thrive.

## Our Values

!!! success "Guiding Principles"

    === "Impact-Driven"
        **Measurable Change**

        We focus on initiatives that create lasting, measurable impact in the communities we serve. Every dollar donated is invested thoughtfully to maximize positive outcomes.

    === "Community-Focused"
        **Local & Global**

        While we support local Seattle-area initiatives, we also extend our reach to communities worldwide, with special attention to underserved populations.

    === "Innovation"
        **Forward-Thinking**

        We embrace innovative approaches to philanthropy, leveraging technology and modern solutions to traditional challenges.

    === "Transparency"
        **Open & Accountable**

        We maintain transparency in our operations and impact reporting, ensuring donors and partners understand how resources are utilized.

    === "Collaboration"
        **Partnership**

        We believe in working together with other organizations, volunteers, and community leaders to amplify our impact.

## Current Initiatives

### The Eton School Foundation

!!! success "Annual Education Support"
    The Li Family Foundation proudly contributes to The Eton School Foundation every year, supporting innovative educational programs and opportunities for students. Our partnership reflects our commitment to excellence in education and nurturing the next generation of leaders.

    **Partnership Since**: Ongoing annual commitment

    **Focus Areas**:
    - Educational excellence programs
    - Student enrichment activities
    - Faculty development
    - Campus improvements

    [:material-school: Learn More About Eton School](https://www.etonschool.org){ .md-button }

### Local Museum Programs

!!! success "Cultural & Arts Support"
    We contribute to multiple local museum programs throughout the Greater Seattle Area, supporting arts education, cultural preservation, and community access to enriching experiences.

    **Supported Institutions**:
    - Seattle Art Museum programs
    - Museum of History & Industry (MOHAI)
    - Pacific Science Center educational initiatives
    - Children's museum family access programs

    **Focus Areas**:
    - Free community access days
    - Educational programming
    - Youth art programs
    - Cultural exhibitions
